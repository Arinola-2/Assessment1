About Me

Hello! My name is Fashola Arinola Karimat, and I'm thrilled to share my journey of transitioning into the tech industry. My academic background began with a National Diploma in Hospitality Management from LASPOTECH. After completing my diploma, I decided to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at the National Open University. Interestingly, my career in the Lagos State Ministry began early in the year, even before I obtained my educational qualifications. I was employed with my SSCE certificate, and my initial role was quite different from what I'm doing today. However, as I progressed in my career, I started to develop an interest in the tech industry.Before I knew it, I was nominated for the She Code training program. Initially, I was hesitant due to my fear of coding and programming languages. However, I decided to take the leap and enroll in the program. I'm glad I made the decision to pursue the She Code Africa training. It's been an eye-opening experience, and I'm excited to continue learning and growing in the tech space.My goal is to become a proficient coder and contribute meaningfully to the tech industry.
